\textbf{Energy Inequality.} Let $D_n := \|\pi_n - \pi ^ *\| ^2 / 2 = D(\pi_n)$ where $D(\pi) = \frac{1}{2} \| \pi - \pi^*\|^2$ is a local Lyapunov function\footnote{Which is what exactly?}.

\textbf{Lemma A.1.} Let $D_n := D(\pi_n)$. Then for any $n \in \mathbb{N}$,

\[
  D_{n+1} \leq D_n + \gamma_n \left < v(\pi_n), \pi_n - \pi^* \right > + \gamma_n \psi _n + \gamma_n \chi_n + \gamma_n^2 \phi_n^2
\]

which gives us an upper bound on the growth of $D_n$. In this equation, error terms are given by XXX.

Some extra definitions:

\[
  \| \Pi \| := \max_{\pi, \pi^\' \in \Pi} \| \pi - \pi^\' \|
\]

\textit{Proof.} By definition of iterates of (PG)\footnote{What is it exactly?}, we have:

\begin{align*}
  D_{n+1} = \frac{1}{2}\|\pi_{n+1} - \pi^* \|^2 &= \frac{1}{2}\|proj_\Pi(\pi_n + \gamma_n \hat{v_n}) - proj_\Pi(\pi^*)\|^2 \\
          &\leq \frac{1}{2}\|\pi_n + \gamma_n \hat{v_n} - \pi^*\|^2 \text{ by the non expanding property of projection} \\
          &= \frac{1}{2}\<\pi_n + \gamma_n \hat{v_n} - \pi^*, \pi_n + \gamma_n \hat{v_n} - \pi^*\> \\
          &= \frac{1}{2} \<\pi_n - \pi^*, \pi_n - \pi^*\> + \frac{1}{2}2 \<\gamma_n \hat{v_n}, \pi_n - \pi^*\> + \frac{1}{2}2 \<\gamma_n \hat{v_n}, \gamma_n \hat{v_n}\> \\
          &= \frac{1}{2}\left\|\pi_n - \pi^*\right\|^2 + \gamma_n \<\hat{v_n}, \pi_n - \pi^*\> + \frac{1}{2}\gamma_n^2\|\hat v_n\|^2 \\
          &= D_n + \gamma_n\< v\<\pi_n
\end{align*}
