Hub Cities

Where we gather

Each hub hosts sprint watch parties, workshops, and community events. Run by local organizers who know their context.

Eastern Europe

Tbilisi

Georgia's post-revolution reform story makes it a natural home for studying institutional transformation.

Latin America

Bogotá

Colombia's peace process, urban innovation, and growing tech scene make it a hub for studying progress amid complexity.

East Africa

Nairobi

East Africa's tech and financial innovation capital. Home to M-Pesa, a booming startup scene, and major research institutions.

Southeast Asia

Ho Chi Minh City

Vietnam's manufacturing miracle and rapid digitization make it one of the most compelling progress stories of the 21st century.

Middle East

Beirut

Despite crisis, Beirut remains the Arab world's intellectual capital — and a powerful case study in institutional fragility.

East Africa

Kigali

Rwanda's state capacity and development ambition make it an essential case — and an increasingly vibrant research base.

Central Asia

Tashkent

Uzbekistan's opening and reform program puts Central Asia on the progress studies map.

West Africa

Lagos

Africa's largest city, biggest economy, and most dynamic tech ecosystem. The ultimate laboratory for studying urbanization and growth.

Latin America

São Paulo

Latin America's economic engine with deep research institutions and a massive fintech sector.

Middle East

Amman

Jordan's stability, refugee integration challenges, and growing tech scene make it a unique research base.

Start a Hub

Your city isn't listed?

We're actively looking for local organizers to launch hubs in new cities. If you're connected to a university, coworking space, research org, or just an enthusiastic group of people interested in progress — we want to hear from you.

Hub organizers get sprint materials, mentorship, a small events budget, and a direct line to the Vox Nova network.

Apply to Start a Hub

What a hub needs

✓ 1-2 local organizers committed to running events
✓ A venue for sprint watch parties (university room, coworking space, café)
✓ Connection to a local community interested in progress and development
✓ Willingness to host at least 3 events per year
